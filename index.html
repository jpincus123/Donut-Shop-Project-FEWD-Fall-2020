Welcome to the doughnut shoppe

Created in 2012, the doughnut shoppe has been a hidden gem of the Rochester region. Its founders, Lara Press-Ellingham and her husband Ron, knew they were onto something when they discovered the what a delightful combination of a hot beverage combined with the sweetness of a donut. They knew right then and there they were hooked and that's when the idea for the doughnut shoppe became a reality. What started out as a tiny little space in the hipster area of downtown Rochester New York, has grown into several locations throughout the region. Offering a wide variety of donuts and other sweets that are paired with the perfect cup of coffee, the doughnut shoppe is guaranteed to brighten your day and turn around any shitty scenario with one sip and a few bites. Come see for yourself and you'll know instantly why this special space is rated one of Rochester's greatest year after year.
